---
title: "Initialize Payment"
description: "Start a payment session and get a checkout URL for your customer"
---

Initialize a transaction to generate a secure checkout URL where your customer completes payment.

## How it works

1. **Send a request** with the customer's email, amount, currency, and payment channel.
2. **Receive a `checkout_url`** and `access_code` in the response.
3. **Redirect the customer** to the checkout URL to complete payment.
4. **Verify the payment** after the customer returns using the transaction reference.

## Key fields

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| `customer_email` | Yes | Customer's email address |
| `amount` | Yes | Amount in naira (e.g. `"5000"` for ₦5,000) |
| `channels` | Yes | `["BANK_TRANSFER"]` |
| `currency` | No | Defaults to `NGN` |
| `redirect_url` | No | Where to send the customer after payment |
| `metadata` | No | Custom object to attach to the transaction |

<Note>
Always verify the payment on your server after the customer returns. Never fulfil an order based on the redirect alone.
</Note>
