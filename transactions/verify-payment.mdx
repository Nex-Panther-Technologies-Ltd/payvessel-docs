---
title: "Verify Payment"
description: "Confirm the status of a payment before fulfilling an order"
---

Always verify a payment server-side before delivering goods or services. Never rely solely on a redirect callback.

## How it works

1. **Collect the transaction reference** from the redirect URL or your database.
2. **Call the verify endpoint** with the reference.
3. **Check the `status` field** — only fulfil the order when `status` is `success`.

## Status values

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `success` | Payment confirmed — safe to fulfil |
| `pending` | Not yet confirmed — wait or retry |
| `failed` | Payment failed — notify the customer |

<Warning>
Verify using your `api-key` and `api-secret` from your server. Never trust client-side confirmation alone.
</Warning>
