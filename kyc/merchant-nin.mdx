---
title: "NIN KYC (Guide)"
description: "Using NIN for merchant onboarding"
---

Use NIN KYC when you want to anchor identity checks on the **national ID system** rather than banking records.

Typical pattern:

1. Collect NIN plus name, date of birth, and phone.
2. Run **basic NIN** for low‑risk onboarding; upgrade to **enhanced NIN** for higher limits or sensitive products.
3. Cross‑check NIN results against BVN or document data when available.

See **API reference → KYC & Risk → Merchant NIN KYC** for endpoint paths and payload examples.
