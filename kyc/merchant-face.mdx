---
title: "Face Verification (Guide)"
description: "How to compare customer faces against existing records"
---

Face verification helps you **link a real person to an existing identity record** and reduce impersonation.

Typical flow:

1. Capture a live face image from your app (camera capture on web or mobile).
2. Choose a source record to compare against (BVN, NIN, or a verified document photo).
3. Call the face‑compare endpoint from **API reference → KYC & Risk → Merchant Face Verification**.
4. Use the match result and similarity score to decide whether to continue onboarding or escalate to manual review.
