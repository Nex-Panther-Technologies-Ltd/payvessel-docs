---
title: "BVN KYC (Guide)"
description: "How and when to use merchant BVN verification"
---

Use BVN KYC when you need a **strong, bank‑grade identity check** for Nigerian customers.

Recommended flow:

1. Collect BVN and basic identity details (name, date of birth, phone).
2. Call **Merchant BVN KYC** in the API reference:
   - Basic BVN for quick checks.
   - Enhanced BVN when you need a full profile.
3. Store the verification result (including reference) with your customer.
4. Combine with document and liveness checks for high‑risk users.

See **API reference → KYC & Risk → Merchant BVN KYC** for endpoints and payloads.
