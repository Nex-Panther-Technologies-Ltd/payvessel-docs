---
title: "Document Verification (Guide)"
description: "Driver’s licence, voter’s card, and international passport checks"
---

Use document verification to **confirm that a customer’s government ID is valid** and consistent with the rest of their profile.

Suggested usage:

- Verify at least one document (driver’s licence, voter’s card, or passport) for medium‑to‑high risk accounts.
- Compare document names and DOB against BVN/NIN and your own records.
- Store document verification references for audits and dispute resolution.

Refer to **API reference → KYC & Risk → Merchant Document Verification** for the exact endpoints and request fields.
