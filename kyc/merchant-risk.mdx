title: "Risk & Scoring (Guide)"
description: "Using blacklist, credit score, and loan feature checks"
---

Risk services help you **decide what customers can do and on what terms**.

Common usage:

- Run **blacklist queries** before enabling high‑risk actions (e.g. large transfers or loan disbursements).
- Use **credit score** results to set limits, pricing, or required collateral.
- Call **loan feature** queries when you need to know what loan products and parameters are appropriate for a customer.

Use these checks alongside BVN/NIN, documents, face, and liveness to design tiered onboarding and dynamic risk‑based limits.

See **API reference → KYC & Risk → Merchant Risk & Scoring** for the individual endpoints.
