Use managed wallets to **hold balances for your business**, fund transfers, and reconcile payouts. A typical flow is:

1. Get or create your business wallet.
2. Check wallet balance before funding transfers.
3. Send single or bulk transfers from the wallet.
4. Query wallet transactions for reconciliation and reporting.

## Key concepts

- **Business wallet**: primary wallet linked to your merchant account, used for most payouts.
- **Available balance**: funds you can spend immediately.
- **Ledger balance**: available balance plus pending transactions.
- **Transactions**: credits (funding, incoming payments) and debits (transfers, fees).
