---
title: "Credit Score Query"
description: "Use credit score queries when your workflow depends on customer risk grading"
---

Credit score queries provide a score-oriented risk signal that can support underwriting, limits, pricing, or access decisions.

## Best For

- Lending decisions
- Tiered product access
- Risk-based pricing or approval logic

## What You Submit

You provide:

- Mobile number
- ID number
- Optional extended metadata

## What You Get Back

The response can include:

- `credit_score`
- `version`
- `request_id`

## How To Use It

- Define score bands that map to approval or review outcomes
- Combine the score with identity verification and blacklist results
- Keep score use consistent with your compliance and product policy
