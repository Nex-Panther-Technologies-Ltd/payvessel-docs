---
title: "Driver's License Verification"
description: "Use driver's license verification when a license is part of your accepted identity set"
---

Driver’s license verification is useful when you accept a Nigerian driver’s license as a supporting or primary document for onboarding.

## Best For

- Document-based KYC
- Multi-document onboarding journeys
- Secondary verification after BVN or NIN

## What You Submit

You submit the license number.

## What You Get Back

The response can include:

- Names
- Gender
- Birth date
- Photo
- License number
- Issue date
- Expiry date

## How To Use It

- Confirm the document exists and is tied to the expected customer
- Check the issue and expiry dates before approval
- Compare the returned photo and names to other identity records

<Tip>
Document verification is strongest when combined with a primary identity anchor such as BVN or NIN.
</Tip>
